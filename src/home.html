Welcome to SalingSapa Coffee, your neighborhood coffee haven where passion for coffee meets community spirit. Founded in 2024, our coffee shop was born out of a love for great coffee and a desire to create a warm, inviting space where everyone feels at home.

Read More

At SalingSapa, we believe that a great cup of coffee can bring people together. That's why we source only the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world, roasting them to perfection right here in our shop. Our baristas are skilled artisans, dedicated to crafting each cup with care, whether it's a bold espresso, a creamy latte, or a refreshing cold brew.
