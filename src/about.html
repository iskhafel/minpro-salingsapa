SalingSapa Coffee began as a dream shared by two lifelong friends, united by their love for coffee and a vision to create a place where people could connect and unwind. In the bustling heart of the city, they found the perfect spot—a cozy corner with just enough space for a handful of tables, but overflowing with potential.


In 2024, after months of planning, tasting, and perfecting their craft, SalingSapa Coffee opened its doors. From the very first day, the shop was more than just a place to grab a quick caffeine fix. It became a hub for the community—a place where students could study, professionals could work, and friends could catch up over a cup of expertly brewed coffee.


Good Quality


What sets apart is our commitment to quality and sustainability. We source our beans from small, ethical farms around the world, ensuring that every cup not only tastes exceptional but also supports the farmers who make it possible. Our roasting process is an art form, carefully balancing time and temperature to bring out the unique flavors of each batch.


Good Space


But this place is about more than just coffee. It’s about creating a space where everyone feels welcome—a place where you can relax, recharge, and feel at home. Whether you’re here to work, socialize, or simply enjoy a moment of peace with your favorite brew, we’re here to make your day just a little bit brighter.


Good People


Come visit us and become a part of our story. Every cup we serve is a testament to our love for coffee and our dedication to bringing people together, one sip at a time.
